DRY - Don't Repeat Yourself


S.O.L.I.D
SRP - Single Responsibility Principle

Basic Salary = 50000
HRA = 35% of basic salary
MA = 15% of basic salary
TA = 20% of basic salary
DA = 30% of basic salary
Earnings = basic salary + HRA + MA + TA + DA

Income Tax = 5% of basic salary
PF = 2% of basic salary
Deductions = Income Tax + PF

Net Payable Salary = Earnings - Deductions
